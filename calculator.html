How This Calculator Works

  • Consolidation: Uses standard amortization formula to calculate monthly payments and total interest. Adds origination fee and monthly program fees.
  • Bankruptcy: Uses a flat cost model: $338 court filing fee + attorney fee + ~$40 for required credit counseling courses.
  • No data is collected. All calculations run entirely in your browser. Nothing is sent to any server. You can verify this by viewing the page source.
